@h3 Singly Linked List
@img https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6d/Singly-linked-list.svg/408px-Singly-linked-list.svg.png
@h4 operations
@starttextarea
1 - insert at a position, insert at start, insert at end
2 - delete at a position, delete at start, delete at end
3 - traverse a linked list from start to end
4 - find an entry in the linked list
5 - find length of the linked list
@endtextarea


@h3 Doubly Linked List
@img https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5e/Doubly-linked-list.svg/610px-Doubly-linked-list.svg.png
@h4 operations
@starttextarea
1 - insert at a position, insert at start, insert at end
2 - delete at a position, delete at start, delete at end
3 - traverse a linked list from start to end, from end to start (reverse order)
4 - find an entry in the linked list
5 - find length of the linked list
@endtextarea
