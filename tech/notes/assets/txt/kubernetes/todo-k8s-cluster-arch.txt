@h4 Nodes
@link https://kubernetes.io/docs/concepts/architecture/nodes/

@pre Kubernetes runs your workload by placing containers into Pods to run on Nodes. A node may be a virtual or physical machine, depending on the cluster. Each node is managed by the control plane and contains the services necessary to run Pods.
